[City Council Proposal Draft]
Proposal: "Green City Center Initiative"
Effective Date: January 1, 2027

Objective: 
To drastically reduce carbon emissions and ease traffic congestion in the downtown commercial zone.

Key Provisions:
1. Complete ban on all internal combustion engine (ICE) vehicles entering the designated "Downtown Core" zone between 7:00 AM and 7:00 PM on weekdays.
2. Delivery trucks must switch to electric fleets to service downtown businesses during these hours.
3. Increased funding for public transit expansion. 

Impact:
While projected to reduce downtown air pollution by 40%, local downtown business associations have warned that foot traffic may decrease and delivery costs will skyrocket, potentially forcing small shops to raise prices or close. Environmental groups strongly support the measure.
