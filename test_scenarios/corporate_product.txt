[Product Launch Overview]
Product Name: SwarmVision Pro AR Glasses
Target Release: Next Quarter 
Target Demo: Tech enthusiasts, early adopters, and productivity power users.

Overview:
SwarmVision Pro features next-generation retinal projection, a 160-degree field of view, and a 24-hour battery life. It is positioned to replace standard smartphones within the next five years.

Pricing Strategy:
Priced at $1,299, the device is aimed at the premium market. Early reviews from beta testers indicate extreme excitement, but some concerns have been raised privately about eye strain after 6+ hours of continuous use.

Marketing Plan:
A massive social media blitz is scheduled for the week leading up to the launch, featuring top tech influencers and a live keynote address.
