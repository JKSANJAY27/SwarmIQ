[Internal Memo]
Date: October 15, 2026
To: All Students and Faculty
From: University Administration

Subject: Cancellation of the Annual Spring Cultural Festival

Due to unprecedented budget constraints and recent concerns regarding campus safety during large-scale gatherings, the University Administration has made the difficult decision to cancel this year's Annual Spring Cultural Festival. 

We understand this event is a highly anticipated tradition that brings together students, alumni, and the local community. However, prioritizing the long-term financial stability and security of our campus must take precedence. 

All student organizations that have paid stall fees will be refunded by the end of the month. We encourage students to find smaller, departmental-level ways to celebrate this spring.

We appreciate your understanding during these challenging times.
